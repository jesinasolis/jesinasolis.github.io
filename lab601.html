Recipe

Ingredient Quantity
Package cream cheese, softened 1 (8 ounce)
Confectioner's sugar 1/2 cup
Vanilla extract 1/2 teaspoon
Fresh strawberries 1 pint
Graham crackers 4 graham crackers
Semi-sweet chocolate chips 1/4 cup

Steps

  1. First, you want to prepare everything. For the strawberries, wash them, then hull and core the middle of the strawberry. Next, crush up graham crackers by putting them in a bag or processing them.
  2. Beat cream cheese, confectioner's sugar, and vanilla extract together in a bowl until smooth. Put filling into a piping bag or resealable bag with a corner snipped off.
  3. Fill cavities of cored strawberries with the filling. Dip the filled side of strawberries in the crushed graham crackers to coat them.
  4. Optional: Melt chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl and drizzle on top of the filled strawberries.
  5. Let the chocolate harden in the refrigerator, then enjoy!
