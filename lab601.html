Recipe

Ingredient Quantity
package cream cheese, soften 1 (8 ounce)
confectioner's sugar 1/2 cup
Vanilla extract 1/2 teaspoon
fresh Strawberries 1 pint
Gramham crackers 4 graham crackers
semi-sweet chocolate chips 1/4 cup

Steps

  1. First you want to prepare everything, for the strawberies wash them then hull and core the middle of the strawberry. Next crush up graham crackers by putting them in a bag or processing them.
  2. Beat cream cheese, confectioners sugar, and vanilla extract together in a bowl until smooth. Put filling into a piping bag or resealable bag with a corner snipped off.
  3. Fill cavities of cored strawberries with the filling. Dip-filled side of strawberries in the crushed graham crackers to coat
  4. optional melt chocolate chips in a microwave safe bowl and drizzle on top of the filled strawberries
  5. let chocolate hardened in refrigerator then enjoy!
